Summary

  1. Determine the categories you will classify images in to. (In the lesson, this was thumbs up/thumbs down.)
    Didn't do the lesson/activity in class? Feel free to do the activity yourself (it takes about 20 minutes) here.
  2. Gather training images of all categories using a WiFi Cam.
  3. Train a model on Google Colab using this notebook.
  4. Upload the model. (See model upload below)
  5. Connect the WiFi Cam to your Arduino and request predictions from your model!
