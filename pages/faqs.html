FAQs

What coordinates does the Vision System use?
The Arena extends 4m in the x-axis, 2m in the y-axis, and theta from -π to π with 0 facing the positive x-axis.
How accurate is the Vision System? How quickly does it respond?
The Vision System is accurate down to 1cm. It responds within 240ms.
How are objects positioned? Will there ever be an object near the rocky terrain, a wall, or the destination?
The four objects will always be positioned after the rocky terrain. Objects can be positioned adjacent to the rocky terrain, or other obstacles. However, obstacles will never be positioned adjacent to a wall or the destination.
How will the OSV know where the destination is?
You can retrieve the destination by calling enes.retrieveDestination().
For more information, check the documentation.
